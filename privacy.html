Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

MemoryBackupApp values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Access

  • Photos and Videos: Access is required to back up your media to your external drive.
  • No other personal data is collected.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not upload your photos or videos to any cloud.
  • We do not sell, share, or analyze your media.
  • All processing happens directly on your iPhone.

3. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed via your Apple ID account. We do not store billing information.

4. Your Rights

You may revoke Photos access at any time in iOS Settings. You may also delete the app to remove all local data.

5. Contact

For questions, email: support@memorybackupapp.com
