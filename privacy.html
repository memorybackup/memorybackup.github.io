Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the MemoryBackup App.

1. Data Access

  • The app requires access to your Photos and Videos in order to back them up to your external storage device.
  • No other personal data is accessed.

2. Data Usage

  • All processing of your photos and videos is performed locally on your device.
  • Your media is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external servers or third parties.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data, media files, or usage information.
  • Your data remains entirely on your device and external storage.

4. Subscriptions

If you choose to subscribe to premium features, payment and subscription management are handled securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

5. Analytics

We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics of any kind.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page and within the app.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support.memorybackup@gmail.com
